THE BACK GARDEN AT THE SIX ©

Nestled within the heart of the serene backwoods, The Back Garden at The Six Ventura, California, offers a tranquil escape. Away from the bustling Main Street, this hidden gem invites you to a harmonious blend of indoor and outdoor spaces. 🌿 Abundant greenery envelops the garden, from floor to ceiling, creating an enchanting atmosphere. Fresh plants and meticulously crafted artificial flowers coexist, adding a touch of whimsy. Whether you’re planning a wedding, a rehearsal dinner, or any special occasion, this venue exudes a rustic yet elegant charm that celebrates the natural beauty of the outdoors. 🌼 With a capacity to accommodate up to 110 people, The Back Garden provides a picturesque setting for your cherished moments. 🌸
